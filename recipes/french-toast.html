French Toast

Description:

All the sweet goodness of a normal piece of toast, with a super-rich spin, this egg-soaked, cinnamony piece of toast will set you on your way to early-onset diabetes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 piece of Texas Toast
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/8 cup milk
  • 1/8 tsp vanilla
  • 1/16 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tbsp maple syrup

Steps:

  1. In a very shallow bowl (think shallow like your soul), whisk together the egg, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  2. Melt butter in small skillet over medium heat.
  3. Dip -- but not soak! -- the slice of bread in the mixture on both sides, then transfer to the skillet.
  4. Cook the toast to golden brown on both sides.
  5. Top with maple syrup and laugh at the fact that you don't have to share.
