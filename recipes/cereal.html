Cereal

Description:

Craving a bowl of processed sugar? Look no further.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Your chosen cereal
  • (Optional) More sugar to taste

Steps:

  1. Pour the desired amount of your chosen cereal in your favorite bowl. If you've elected to add more sugar, do so now.
  2. Add milk to desired level. There is some debate over the order of milk and cereal in the bowl, and honestly it probably doesn't matter. Except it does and if you do it any other way, you're wrong.
  3. (Optional) Drink remaining milk after cereal is gone. Some people feel very strongly about this because if you don't drink this milk, you're apparently wasting it and therefore a terrible person. Nevermind processing and other considerations involved in the life choices of eating the processed sugar, if you feel so inclined, drink the extra sugary milk remaining in the bowl after you've eaten your cereal.
